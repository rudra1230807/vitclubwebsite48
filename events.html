VISHWAKARANDAK

Vishwakarandak is one of the most prestigious inter-college tech fests, bringing together brilliant minds to showcase their skills in innovation, robotics, coding, and design.

VISHWOTSAV

Vishwotsav, an esteemed tradition of VIT, is a three-day fest filled with music, dance, and culture, crafting unforgettable memories with a lively and energetic atmosphere.

FRESHERS PARTY

Freshers' party is a glorious welcoming ceremony organized by EPEC for our freshmen that marks the exciting beginning of a new academic journey. With performances that showcase the diverse talents of the students we set the stage for a memorable college experience. As reputed clubs of our very own campus coax the newcomers with their legacy, our freshers step up and announce their arrival and embrace the exhilarating ride that awaits them.

VISHWA TECH SPECTRA

Welcome to Vishwa Tech Spectra (VTS) – a three-day inter college fest that transforms VIT Pune into a hub of innovation, discovery, and tech-driven excitement. This celebration of knowledge and skill invites students to dive into a diverse array of technical competitions, hands-on workshops, and dynamic challenges that blend learning with excitement. From coding contests and robotics challenges to insightful workshops led by industry experts, VTS offers something for every tech enthusiast. It’s a journey of growth, collaboration, and creativity, where minds come together to explore the future of technology in an environment that’s both energizing and informative.
